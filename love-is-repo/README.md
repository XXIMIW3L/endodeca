## Collection Description (short)
*love is* renders care as designed behavior—architectural light and measured tenderness, composed to uplift and energize. The image breathes at a humane tempo, tuned to **432 Hz**, inviting the heart to rise without fanfare.

## Work Notes (for catalog)
- **Source imagery:** the visual composition began with photography/videography of a **real-life nighttime starry sky**.  
- **Post / image discipline:** DaVinci Resolve, CapCut, Procreate.  
- **Capture:** Apple iPhone.  
- **Audio:** composed and produced in **PreSonus Studio One**, tuned to **432 Hz**.  
- **Intent:** composed to **uplift and energize** without resorting to spectacle; devotional without doctrine.
